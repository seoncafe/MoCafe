2014-10-25, This is how the figures made for the paper.
1. ds9 -colorbar vertical -colorbar fontsize 16 -3d -3d az 30 -3d el 30 -3d border color white M010_001.fits.gz -zoom to fit
ds9 -width 420 -colorbar vertical -colorbar fontsize 16 -3d -3d az 30 -3d el 30 -3d border color white M010_001.fits.gz -zoom to fit -cmap bb -psprint
2. 3D rotation angles: azimuth = 30, elevation = 30
3. 3D border color -> white
4. color = bb
5. adjust the picture frame size
6. zoom -> fit
7. file -> print
